Electric Vehicle Ownership Continues to Rise in Washington

The Biden Administration is trekking towards the goal for 56% of new vehicles to be electric by 2032. The efforts have worked as sales have quadrupled.


Particularly, in the State of Washington, purchases of electric and hybrid cars continue daily. With the help of the state's incentives on top of Biden's, the zero-net emmissions levels are more attractive.
